Q: What can I do with JoyCompiler?
A: You can follow you favorite Tv shows/ Movies/ etc..

Q: How to get started on JoyCompiler?
A: [1] Search your favorite category and genre or search the specific event you want by name.
    [2] Add the event to you watchlist.

Q: Can I delete events from my watchlist?
A: Of course you can!
    Just select the event you want to delete and press delete.

Q: I've encounterd a bug! can I report it?
